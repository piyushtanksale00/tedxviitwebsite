Our Previous Events

KAIZEN

TEDxVIIT 2020

Theme – The theme for this event was “Kaizen” i.e “Change for the better”. We all know that to excel is to change and the change helps you grow, expand and establish. All one has to do is move a little.

The 2nd edition of TEDxVIIT. Though the year wasn’t good for the world as it faced a global pandemic, our event was a great success. The event was conducted online on 31st October 2020.



Speakers -

1.Vihari Kanukollu: He is the CEO and co-founder of ‘Urban Kissan’. It is a startup that provides a farm controlling software system and empowers people to grow their own safe, fresh and high-quality food.

2.Samira Davalbhakta: A medical student who believes that productivity isn’t something that we gain after working 24/7 but rather by working systematically.

3.Gautami Deshpande: An engineer turned actor, this Marathi actress is a graduate of VIIT, Pune. Her determination has taken her a long way, her life experiences have inspired many to achieve their dreams.

4.Mukesh Chhabra: A truly talented director, actor and all-rounder who has directed amazing movies like ‘Dil Bechara’. His journey from casting director to directing an everlasting movie is remarkable.

5.Kiran Kalamdani: Founder of Kimaya Architects, is a well-established architect who contributes his skills to the conservation of Indian monuments.

6.Amit Kalantri: A well-known mentalist and magician has always left the audience stunned with his skills.

7.Anup Gaikwad aka Wah da fu: He is a beatboxer and producer, who performed at the event. As it said rappers can capture important life lessons with catchphrases and one-liners in a very inspiring way, his performance was loved by many.

8.Shreya Chaudhry: An actress who believes ‘acting is not begin famously but it's about exploring the human soul’, got fame for her lead role in the Amazon Prime series Bandish Bandits, shared her journey of becoming a celebrity.

9.Shridhar Aiyer: An author and inspiration speaker who believes ‘To get inspired is very easy but to inspire is difficult’ inspired the audience by his skills.

press play

TEDxVIIT 2019

The theme for the year was ‘press play’, with this theme we presented a new outlook on life that aims at promulgating the idea of not just going and growing through the tough phases but also glowing through it. A theme aimed at proposing pressing the ‘play' button in your life when life is ‘paused’ or ‘stopped’.

The very 1st edition of TEDxVIIT, this event took place on 25th March 2019. It was our colleges’ first “TEDx” event. The event was conducted at Phoenix Market city, liberty Square, Pune. This first edition of TEDxVIIT was a huge success.



Speakers -

1.Mr. Subhash Talekar: The president of Mumbai Dabbawaalas Association. The man leads an army of 5000 dabbawaalas and caters food over 2 lakh Mumbaikars.

2.Tanya Khanijow: A perfect example of the word ‘wanderlust’, she is a travel vlogger who has explored beaches, mountains and everything in between. She left the mainstream 9 – 5 life and begin her journey of blogging and vlogging.

3.Erika Abrams: She is the co-founder of Animal Aid Unlimited and the Federation of Indian animal protection organizations who took up commendable initiatives of Animal Aid.

4.Chef. Amninder Sandhu: She is a world-renowned chef who represented India on Netflix’s culinary competition ‘The Final Table’ and inspired women of our country to follow their passion.

5.Suswar Ganu: He is the director at Deutsche bank, who is a multitalented person, acing the technical and financial streams and has accomplished the “Millennial Dreams”.

6.Ms Dedeepya Reddy: The founder of “Chal Rang de” has added colours to Mumbai's slum landscape and has turned into a true hero.

7.Abhishek Mohurley: He is a renowned author and founder of 2 startups. He believes in giving a voice to the truth.

8.Mr Vivek Patil: He was one of the finalists of “India’s Got Talent” who is loved for his light, sand and speed art.
