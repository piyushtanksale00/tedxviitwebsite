About Us

ideas worth spreading

What is TED?

TED stands for “Technology, Entertainment and Design” was first started as a 4-day conference in California around 30 years ago. It’s widely known as a non-profit organization that emphasises the slogan “Ideas worth spreading”. The organization is devoted to spreading new ideas in the form of short and influential talks. The ultimate aim of this global community is to spread ideas, which believed that ideas can bring revolution, break stereotypes and change one's life. To cut it short, TED has an agenda to bring great ideas to light.

What is TEDx?

TEDx is a popular initiative which is been created in the essence of the mission of “Ideas worth spreading” of TED. TEDx events are organized by communities involving focused & passionate individuals who work on their ideas to make them big and express them through their talk, what inspired them to work on it and spread their spark in the community. This event is organized independently under the free license of TED, it includes live and recorded talks.

“If everyone is moving forward together, then success takes care of itself” – Henry Ford.

TEDxVIIT Community

A saying which completely defines our team. Our team at TEDxVIIT is a set of curious, passionate, and young engineers who believe that ideas are as powerful as a revolution. We hope to help in exposing our community to a wide spectrum of ideas that would inspire them to innovate and create a world we all aspire to live in.

We collectively work to create an experience that essentially reflects the core TED philosophy of ‘Ideas Worth Spreading’ and makes sure that the event creates a lasting impact on the lives of the community members. We believe ideas have the power to transform an entire generation’s thought process and our team at TEDxVIIT is proud to be able to contribute in bringing change.
